About Orance

Orance is a social media platform which aims to connect artists all over the world. Unlike other platforms, it provides a headstart to all the people passionate about their art. With a group of people having same intrest it will be much more easier for everyone to showcase their talent to the one who can judge them well.

It provides a social platform where user can share their art, see other people's work and can also find some best artworks which can help them to improve their skills. There is also an AI model which enables artists to come up with some unique ideas. Using Image merging user can merge two different images together to obtain a unique image, which can provide some cool ideas to users to work upon.
